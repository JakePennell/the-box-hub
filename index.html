About Us

Welcome to The Box Hub! We are a boxing club based in South London for beginner boxers to hone their talents. We have several top-class instructors, each with a wealth of experience in and out of the ring. We run a number of classes through the week aimed at improving your core boxing techniques and abilities, fitness and strength training and sparring sessions.

Our warm and welcoming atmosphere is perfect for you, whatever your goals. Fitness, Boxing ability, weight loss or self-defence. Our members love how our classes make them feel more fit energetic and confident. Sign up today and get your first class absolutely free!
